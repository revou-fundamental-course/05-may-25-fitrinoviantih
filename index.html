Introduction About Yulje

Yulje Medical Center is one of the big hospital businesses from professor Ahn Jeong-Won's family based in seoul. Yulje Medical Center is a hospital committed to delivering high-quality medical services, advanced clinical research, and comprehensive medical education. Yulje is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and a multidisciplinary team of experts, ensuring patient-centered care across a wide range of specialties.

Until now, Yulje Medical Center has several branches namely Jeongno, Sokcho, Sejong, and Songdo. The center offers a comprehensive range of specialized services, including cardiology, neurology, oncology, surgery, and obstetrics, ensuring holistic and integrated treatment for patients. This hospital emphasizes innovation in healthcare delivery, ethical medical practices, and continuous professional development. With a reputation for clinical excellence and a strong academic foundation, Yulje Medical Center serves as a benchmark for modern medical institutions in South Korea
